Hi, I'm Jerry. I grew up in steel town central. Hard labor, a good dose of sweat, and the satisfaction of a good days' work top my list of sweet pleasures. I got started in photography by accident. I used to carry around an old WWII journalists' camera and snap shots like I was documenting the 'wild west'. A passing master of the trade saw my tinkering and published it. With his encouragement I cultivated the heart of my upbringing along with an eye for persuasive documentation. I hope to share that with you.

I've spent twenty years building businesses, including my own. As an entrepeneur, I know the path to success.
As a photographer, I know how to get you in a good light.
As your new best friend, I'm ready to take on your project and stop at nothing to get your project Done!
